About Mark

As an artist, Mark started his journey in 2016 at the Enniskerry School of art in Co.Wicklow and later the Burren College of Art Co.Galway.

In 2018 he Joined the Kilmanin Art gallery in Wicklow. Periodically, you can find his works in the Peoples Art exhibit displayed on the railings at St. Stephens Green in Dublin city center.

Mark paints at weekly sessions in the Signal Arts Center Bray with the Papillion Art Group and he has joined a group of 3 Called "The Wicklow Mates" together they exhibit their works on the railings in Wicklow town with the Kilmantin Arts exhibit displayed on the railings in Wicklow town.
Mark's artworks are of oil, acrylic and ink, ranging from Quirkily Cows, Sheep, and Dogs, Landscape Provence and Africa, with his most current works being ladies and faces.
