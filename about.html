About Mark

As an artist, Mark started his journey in 2016 at the Enniskerry School of art in Co. Wicklow and later the Burren College of Art Co.Galway.

In 2018 he Joined the Kilmantin Arts Gallery in Wicklow Town. As a part of the Papillion Art Group, Mark paints weekly at the Signal Arts Center Bray.

In 2020 he joined a group of 3 called "The Wicklow Mates" and together, they exhibit their works along the railings in the heart of Wicklow Town (Sign up for the newsletter to stay informed of upcomming dates). Periodically, you can find his works at the Peoples Art exhibit on the railings at St. Stephens Green in Dublin City Centre. Mark's artworks consist of oil, acrylic and ink, range from Quirkily Cows, Sheep, and Dogs to Landscape Provence and Africa, with his most current works of ladies and faces.
